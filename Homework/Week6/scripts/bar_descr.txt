Quality of support network:     
It's a measure of perceived social network support. The indicator is based on the question: 
If you were in trouble, do you have relatives or friends you can count on to help you whenever you need them, or not?    
The indicator considers the respondents who respond positively.

Educational attainment:
Educational attainment considers the number of adults aged 25 to 64 holding at least an upper secondary degree 
over the population of the same age, as defined by the OECD-ISCED classification.
